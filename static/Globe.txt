Globe internation Lts is a global producer and distributor specialising in purpose-built appareal, footwear and skateboard hardgoods (decks, wheels, trucks, etc) brands for teh boardsports, street fashion and workwear markets. The company was founded in 1985 by three Australian brothers. Globe International's core business is divided between proprietary brands, licensed brands and distributed brands, and its products are sold in more than 100 countries worldwide. The currently pro skater team includes Rodney Mullen, Mark Appleyard, David Gonzalez, Chris Haslam, Ryan Decenzo, Paul Hart, Sammy Montano and Danny Leon.
