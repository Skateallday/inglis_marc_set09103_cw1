Independent Truck Company is a skateboard truck manufacturer based in Santa Cruz, California. Established in 1978 the company is owned by NHS,Inc. and sponsors an extensive list of team riders. The trucks are manufactured in the San Francisco and in China by Ermico Enerprises, In. and only dedicated skateboard truck foundry in teh US. The team includes, but is not limited to: Tony hawk, Chris Haslam, Andrew Reynolds, David Gonzalez, Ben Raemers, Leticia Bufoni, Geoff Rowley, Steve Alba, Pedro barros and many, many others.
