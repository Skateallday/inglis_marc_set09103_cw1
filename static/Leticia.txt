Leticia Bufoni is a professional street skateboarder from Brazilian. She was born on the 13th of April, 1993 in Sao Paulo. At the age of 14 she came to the Southern California X Games and while skating at the Element Skate Camp she made a deal with her Dad to stay in California to persure her dream of becoming a professional. She is ranked the number 1 in women's street skateboarding by the World Cup Skate four years in a row and became the only female athlete to win three X Games Gold Medals in teh same year. Forbes named her one of The Most Powerful Women In International Sport for 2018(#25) and recently made SPorts Pro Media's list for The World's Most Marketable Athletes for 2018 (#42).
