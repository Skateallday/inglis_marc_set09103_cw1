Girl is a skateboarding distribution company base in Torrance, California. The company houses the following brands: Girl Skateboards, Chocolate Skateboards, Royal Skateboard Trucks, Ruby Republic and Fourstar CLothing. The brand was founded in 1993 after a selection of team riders from the World Industries distribution company decided to found their own brand. Two of these main figures are Mike Carrol and Rick Howard.<p>Currently, the pro skaters that are signed for the brand are: Mike Carroll, Rick Howard, Jerson Wilson, Rick McCrank, Brandon Biebel, Sean Amlto, Mike Mo Capaldi, Cory Kenndey and Andrew Brophy.
