Plan B Skateboards is a skateboarding company based in Costa Mesa, California, United States, founded by Mike Ternasky, Jackson Kontzer, and Brian Johnson in 1991. Later in 2005, professional skateboarders Danny Way and Colin McKay, would play a big role in the brand's revival. Plan B sells both soft and hard goods, including skateboard decks and wheels, jeans, hooded jumpers, and jackets.
