Eric Koston was born on the 29th of April, 1975 in Bangkok, Thailand. Now a resident of of Los Feliz, California, Koston is part owner of "The Berrics", a private indoor skatepark that is a hub of skateboard-related media, alongside Steve Berra. Koston has also featured in both the Tony Hawk's and Skate video game series and won 1st place in 26 professional level competitions.
