Almost Skateboards is a US Skateboard company founded by professional skateboarders and business partners Daewon Song and Rodney Mullen. The Company manufactures skateboard decks using 7-ply, 8-ply and carbon fiber construction while the decks are bound with resin epoxy glue. The Current team professionals are: Rodney Mullen, Daewon Song, Cooper Wilt, Christoph Wildgrube, Youness Amrani, Chris Haslam Yuri Facchini and Mitchie Brusco.
