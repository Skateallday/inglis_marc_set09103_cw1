Supra is an American Shoe Brand Founded in California by Angel Cabada, in partnership with pro skaters such as Chad Muska, Jim Greco and Erik Ellington. The company has been described as an "underground idea" shared between five people that became a "crazy explosion". The team consists of: Dee Ostrander, Jim Greco, Lizard King, Tom Penny, Spencer hamilton, Chad Muska and Antwuan Dixon.
