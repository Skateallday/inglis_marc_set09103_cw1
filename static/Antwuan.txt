Antwuan Willis Dixon (born August 19, 1988)[1] is a professional skateboarder. Dixon was alternately featured in the Baker Skateboards video Baker Has a Deathwish, then rode for Deathwish skateboards.In 2013, Dixon received a three year jail sentence after violating his probation, and was charged with battery, vandalism, and child endangerment after an argument at a convenience store escalated to violence. Dixon served his sentence and is now living in Los Angeles.
