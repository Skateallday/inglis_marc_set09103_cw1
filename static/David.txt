David Gonzalez (born 9 August 1990) is a Colombian professional skateboarder. In December 2012 he was named Thrasher magazine's "Skater of the Year". As of January 2013, Gonzalez is a professional member of the Flip Skateboards team the company has released numerous signature model skateboard decks that feature Gonzalez's name, including "P2"-technology decks.
